    Floppy
    Federated Learning on a Peer to Peer system

    Protecting Data in Distributed Deep Neural Networks: In today's privacy-conscious world, safeguarding data shared with third parties is crucial. When sensitive information enters distributed Deep Neural Networks, there is a genuine risk of privacy violations.

    Novel Approach - Floppy: Introducing Floppy, an innovative algorithm that implements asynchronous Federated Learning and Secure Multi-Party Computation (SMPC) to ensure privacy during collaborative training.

    Resilient and Efficient: Floppy's asynchronous nature makes it resilient against slower nodes, ensuring a smooth training process while preserving data privacy.

    Real-World Evaluation: We evaluate Floppy, along with other techniques, using iid and non-iid image datasets to analyze their impact on computer vision tasks, demonstrating its effectiveness in maintaining data privacy.
